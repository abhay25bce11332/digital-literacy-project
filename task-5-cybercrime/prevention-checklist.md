# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or approving fake payment requests
- Always verify the identity of callers before sharing any information
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities
- Do not share personal information on social media

# Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report any cybercrime immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24×7)
